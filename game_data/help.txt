COMMANDS:
  Movement:    NORTH (N), SOUTH (S), EAST (E), WEST (W), UP (U), DOWN (D)
               or GO <direction>
  Look around: LOOK (L)
  Examine:     EXAMINE <item> (X <item>)
  Take/Drop:   TAKE <item>, DROP <item>
  Use:         USE <item>
  Combine:     COMBINE <item> WITH <item>
  Inventory:   INVENTORY (I)
  Score:       SCORE
  Save/Load:   SAVE, LOAD
  Quit:        QUIT (Q)

TIPS:
  - Examine everything! Items often have important details.
  - Some doors require keycards or special actions to open.
  - If it's dark, find a light source first.
  - Pay attention to computer terminals and notes.
